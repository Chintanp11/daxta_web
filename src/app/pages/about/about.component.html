One in fourty-four children are diagnosed with an autism spectrum disorder in the United States (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, 2021). Interventions based on the science of applied behavior analysis (ABA) are the evidence-based best-practice treatment for autism spectrum disorders. Founded in 2018, DAXTA is a growing company with the mission to globally elevate accessibility and quality of behavior analytic treatment by increasing the effectiveness and capacity of clinical service providers. We do this through our software, trainings, and by providing clinical supervision as well as consultation for ABA clinical service providers.
